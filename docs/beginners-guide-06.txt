====================================================================
 6. THE TABLE AND SESSIONS
====================================================================

The TABLE is where actual play happens. Click "Launch" on your Story page to get there. The Table fills the whole screen — no sidebar.


THE HEADER BAR
--------------
Across the top, left to right:

  Campaign name and setting badge
  Start Session / End Session       (GM)
  Session number badge              (when active)
  Tactical Map / Campaign Map toggle
  Share Map                         (GM — pushes the tactical map to players)
  Start Combat / End Combat         (GM, requires active session)
  Checks ▾                          (Perception, Gut Instinct, First Impression, Group, Opposed, Recruit)
  Community ▾                       (Chapter 9)
  Dashboard
  Share                             (copy invite link)
  Overlay / Inline toggle           (character sheet display mode)
  Restore                           (GM — heal everyone)
  Sessions                          (history)
  Back / Exit


THE LEFT PANEL — LOGS AND CHAT
------------------------------
Three views, toggled at the top of the panel:

  LOGS   — every dice roll, action, and combat event in chronological order, color-coded.
  CHAT   — in-character messaging.
  BOTH   — combined.

Type a message and press Enter to send.


THE CENTER — THE MAP
--------------------
Either the Campaign Map (with pins — Chapter 3) or the Tactical Map (the grid for combat — Chapter 7). Toggle between them with the header button. Players see whatever the GM has shared.

Click a character or NPC token (or their entry in the left panel) to open their character sheet. INLINE mode renders the sheet in the center; OVERLAY mode floats it on top.

THE RIGHT PANEL
---------------
The GM sees:
  NPCs          — full NPC roster (Chapter 10).
  Assets        — campaign pins, drag to reorder.
  GM Notes      — private notes, auto-saved.
  Player Notes  — submissions from players.

Players see:
  NPCs          — only revealed NPCs.
  Assets        — only revealed pins.
  Notes         — your own session notes.


STARTING A SESSION (GM)
-----------------------
Click "Start Session" in the header. The session counter ticks up and every player gets notified. Until a session is active, you can't roll dice or start combat.


DICE ROLLING
------------
With a session active, click any attribute, skill, or weapon on a character sheet or NPC card to open the roll modal. The formula is:

    2d6 + Attribute Modifier (AMod)
        + Skill Modifier      (SMod)
        + Conditional Mod     (CMod — situational)

Pick a CMod from the dropdown (0 by default; the modal lists relevant ones based on your situation). Optionally spend an Insight Die. Click Roll.

OUTCOMES (the standard XSE ladder):

  1+1    Low Insight       Critical failure. +1 Insight Die. Weapons may jam.
  0–3    Dire Failure      Very bad result.
  4–8    Failure           Standard miss.
  9–13   Success           Standard hit.
  14+    Wild Success      Exceptional success.
  6+6    High Insight      Critical success. +1 Insight Die.

Both Low Insight (1+1) and High Insight (6+6) are matched-dice events — they grant an Insight Die in addition to the outcome.


INSIGHT DICE
------------
Earned on Low or High Insight rolls. Stored on your character. PCs only — NPCs only have them if they're the Antagonist type.

Spend one before any roll for ONE of:
  - Roll 3d6 (keep best two) instead of 2d6.
  - Add +3 to your CMod.

You can also spend ALL of your Insight Dice at once to survive a mortal wound (Chapter 8).


ENDING A SESSION (GM)
---------------------
Click "End Session." A modal opens for:

  Session Summary       — what happened this session.
  Next Session Notes    — what's queued for next time.
  Cliffhanger           — the one-line tease.

These get stored in your Story's Sessions history.


SESSION HISTORY
---------------
"Sessions" in the header shows every session you've run, with summaries, cliffhangers, and roll counts.
