====================================================================
 3. THE WORLD MAP
====================================================================

Two maps exist in The Tapestry: the global WORLD MAP and each Story's CAMPAIGN MAP. They use the same engine and most of the same controls.


THE WORLD MAP — /map
--------------------
Click "The World" in the sidebar. You'll see an interactive map centered on North America — that's the world of Distemper.

CONTROLS
  - Pan: click and drag.
  - Zoom: scroll wheel, or the +/− buttons.
  - Search: top-right — type a place or address and click a suggestion to fly there.
  - Map style: switch between Satellite, Topo, Street, Voyager, Humanitarian, Positron, or Dark from the buttons in the top-right.

[screenshot: map with style toggle highlighted]

PINS PANEL (right side; toggle with "Pins")
  PUBLIC tab    — every approved world pin, organized into folders by category. Click a folder to expand. Click a pin to fly to it on the map and read its notes and images.
  MY PINS tab   — pins you placed. Edit, delete, or toggle public/private.
  CAMPAIGN tab  — pins from any Story you're in that the GM has revealed.

The eye icon next to each category folder hides or shows that whole category on the map.

PLACING A PIN
  Click empty map space. A form opens — fill in title, notes, and one or more categories. Optionally upload images. Save.

  As a Survivor, your pin goes to the moderation queue and shows as a Rumor (❓) until a Thriver reviews it (see Chapter 2). As a Thriver, it goes live immediately.


THE CAMPAIGN MAP
----------------
Each Story has its own map, accessible from the Table (Chapter 6). The campaign map has the same controls as the world map, plus a few extra GM tools:

  - Click empty map space to place a campaign pin (no moderation).
  - Drag pins to reposition.
  - Show / Hide a pin to control whether players see it.
  - Link a pin to a tactical scene — players can then double-click the pin to open that scene (Chapter 7).
  - Promote a pin to the world map; it goes to moderation as a Rumor for global visibility.

Players see only revealed pins. Click any pin to read its notes and view its images.

Map style and search work the same as on the world map. The GM picks the default map style and starting location when creating the Story.
