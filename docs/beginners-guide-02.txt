====================================================================
 2. PINS, NOTIFICATIONS, AND ROLES (GM AND PLAYER)
====================================================================


PINS
----
A pin is a labeled point on a map. Pins exist in two places.

WORLD-MAP PINS are placed on the global world map (Chapter 3) and become visible to everyone once approved — see "Moderation" below.

CAMPAIGN-MAP PINS are placed inside a Story by the GM and only visible to that Story's players, and only when the GM has shown the pin. Pins are hidden by default.

Every pin has:
  - Title and notes
  - One or more categories (changes the icon on the map)
  - A latitude / longitude
  - Optional uploaded images

Pin categories — and their map icons:

    Rumor (❓)         Location           Residence
    Business           Church             Government
    Transport          Hospital           Military
    Person             Danger             Resource
    Medical            Group              Animals
    Community          Distemper Timeline Settlement

The "Rumor" category is the special one — see "Moderation" below and Chapter 12.

MODERATION (WORLD-MAP PINS ONLY)
--------------------------------
When a Survivor places a pin on the world map, it goes into a moderation queue and shows on the map as a Rumor (❓) until a moderator reviews it. After review, they either approve it and it goes live with whatever category the submitter chose, or reject it and it goes back to the author.

Admin-placed pins skip moderation and go live immediately. Campaign-map pins skip moderation entirely — they're private to the Story.


NOTIFICATIONS
-------------
Click the bell icon in the user panel to open your notification list.

You'll get a notification for things like:
  - New Player joined your Story
  - Player Left your Story
  - Session Opened (your GM started a session)
  - Module Updated (a Rumor you've subscribed to has a new version — see Chapter 12)
  - Pin Approved or Rejected (your world-map pin was reviewed)
  - New Survivor signed up (Admins only)
  - Pin Submitted, awaiting moderation (Admins only)

Click any notification to jump to it. Use "Mark all read" to clear the red dot, or "Delete all" to wipe the list.


ROLES — INSIDE A STORY
----------------------
Every Story has exactly one GAME MASTER (GM) and any number of PLAYERS:

  GAME MASTER (GM) — the person who created the Story. The GM:
    - Creates and edits all NPCs, pins, tactical scenes, and handouts.
    - Starts and ends sessions.
    - Starts and ends combat.
    - Decides what's revealed to players (NPCs and pins are hidden by default).
    - Has full edit access to everything in the Story.
    - Can launch the world map for other players.

  PLAYER — anyone who has joined the Story. A player:
    - Brings ONE of their characters to the table.
    - Rolls dice for that character.
    - Sees only revealed NPCs and pins.
    - Can place tokens for their own character on the tactical map (but not anyone else's).
    - Can submit notes to the GM.

A player can be a GM in another Story at the same time — your role is per-Story, not per-account.
