====================================================================
 1. NAVIGATING THE SITE
====================================================================

When you sign in you land on the World Map. The site has two parts: the LEFT SIDEBAR (always visible — the only navigation) and the MAIN AREA, where every page renders.

[screenshot: full site with sidebar on left, map on right]


THE SIDEBAR
-----------
At the top, a green "Survivors present: N" counter appears when other players are online.

  USER PANEL
    Your username and three icons:
      - 📨  Messages
      - 🔔  Notifications (red dot if anything is unread)
      - 🔥  The Campfire shortcut

  MAIN NAV (the main destinations)
    A Guide to the Tapestry  — the welcome / onboarding page.
    The World                — the global world map (Chapter 3).
    My Survivors             — your characters (Chapter 4).
    My Stories               — your campaigns (Chapter 5).
    My Communities           — communities you're in (Chapter 9).
    The Campfire             — community hub (Chapter 11).
    Rumors                   — published adventure content (Chapter 12).

  SURVIVORS — the four ways to make a character (Chapter 4):
    Creating a Survivor      — tutorial page.
    Backstory Generation     — a life-pathing method.
    Quick Character          — a fast, truncated build.
    Random Character         — produces an instant character.
    Paradigms                — pick from 12 archetypes and then customize.

  TOOLS (Admins only — internally referred to as Thrivers)
    Moderation Queue, Resize Portraits, Rescale Tactical Scenes, Logs, Copy Map Position, Publish Module from Snapshot.


THE MAIN AREA
-------------
Specific areas are covered in their own chapters. The sidebar is consistent across most pages. A few areas hide it to use the full screen, such as the Table page.
