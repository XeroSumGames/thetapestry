====================================================================
11. THE CAMPFIRE
====================================================================

The Campfire is the cross-Story community hub. Click "The Campfire" in the sidebar.

It's a single page with tabs:

  LOOKING FOR GROUP   (default tab)
  FORUMS
  WAR STORIES
  TIMESTAMPS

All require login but no campaign membership.


LOOKING FOR GROUP
-----------------
A bulletin board for finding GMs and players.

Post a listing as either:
  - GM seeking players, or
  - Player seeking game.

Each post has a title, body, optional setting tag (campaign flavor), and an optional schedule note.

Other people click "I'm Interested" to express interest. As the post author, you see a roster of who's interested and can DM them directly — or, if you're a GM, send them a Story invite. A Share button pushes the post to Reddit, Twitter, Facebook, or copies a direct link.

You can edit and delete your own posts. You can't see other people's interest rosters.


FORUMS
------
Threaded discussions organized by category — Lore, Rules, Session Recaps, General.

  - Click "+ New Thread" to start one. Title and body.
  - Click any thread to read it; reply at the bottom.
  - Edit and delete your own posts.
  - Threads sort by most-recent reply.

Pinned threads (set by Thrivers) float to the top. Locked threads accept no new replies.


WAR STORIES
-----------
A cross-Story feed for session highlights — the time the party blew up the wrong building, the time someone rolled double-6 on the Recruitment Check, etc.

  - Title and body.
  - Optional campaign tag so readers know which Story it came from.
  - Attach images and PDFs.
  - Edit and delete your own posts.


TIMESTAMPS
----------
A utility tool for cross-timezone scheduling. Pick a date, time, and timezone — the page returns a Discord-compatible token (looks like `<t:1234567890:F>`). Paste it in Discord and it renders as the local time for every viewer. Six format options plus raw Unix seconds.
